Plate Speller — Support

About the app

Plate Speller is a word game where each round gives you a three-letter license plate. Your job is to type a word that contains those three letters in order — not necessarily consecutively. You solve 10 plates as fast as you can. Skipping a plate adds a time penalty.

Daily Challenge

Every day, all players get the same 10 plates. Your time is ranked against everyone else who plays that day. You only get one daily challenge per day.

Practice

Play as many rounds as you want with randomly generated plates. Practice runs are not saved.

Getting help

If something isn't working, try:

  • Pull to refresh on the Friends or Profile tab to re-sync data.
  • Force quit and reopen the app if game state looks wrong.
  • Sign out and back in from Profile → Profile Settings if your account looks confused.
