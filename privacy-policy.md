# Plate Speller — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 13, 2026_

Plate Speller ("the app," "we," "us") is committed to keeping your data private and secure. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how to remove it.

## What we collect

When you sign in with Apple, the app stores the following on our backend (Supabase):

- **A user identifier.** Apple provides a unique, anonymous identifier that lets us recognize you across sessions. It is not your Apple ID or email.
- **Your email address (optional).** Only if you choose "Share My Email" during Sign in with Apple. If you pick "Hide My Email," we receive Apple's private relay address instead.
- **Your display name (optional).** Apple may provide your first and last name on your first sign-in. You can edit or remove this in Profile Settings.
- **A username ("handle") you choose.** Used so friends can find you.
- **Your game activity.** Times, skipped plates, words you submitted, and streaks for each daily challenge you complete.
- **Your friends list.** The unique identifiers of users you've added as friends.

We do **not** collect:

- Your location
- Your contacts or photos
- Your device identifiers for tracking
- Analytics for advertising or third-party purposes

## How we use it

- To let you sign in and access your profile across devices.
- To show your stats, daily history, and streak.
- To let friends find you and compare times on the Friends tab.
- To revoke your Apple sign-in when you delete your account, as required by Apple.

We do **not** sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for tracking across other apps or websites.

## Where data is stored

All user data is stored in Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

## Deleting your data

You can delete your account at any time:

1. Open the app.
2. Go to Profile → Profile Settings → Delete Account.
3. Type DELETE to confirm.

This permanently removes your profile, all your daily runs, your friendships, and your Apple sign-in connection. The deletion is immediate and irreversible.

## Children's privacy

Plate Speller does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete the account.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If we do, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after a change indicates acceptance.
